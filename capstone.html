SlugSpot: Efficient Parking Spot Detection

Revolutionizing parking experience on university campuses

Project Overview

The SlugSpot project aims to solve parking challenges on university campuses through innovative sensor technology. Utilizing ultrasonic and inductive sensors, SlugSpot detects the presence of parked vehicles and transmits this information to a central system for easy access via a web interface.

Methodology

SlugSpot employs a combination of inductive and ultrasonic sensors for accurate car detection. Data is transmitted using radio-frequency modules and processed to provide real-time parking availability information on the website.

Outcomes

The outcome is a user-friendly system that increases parking spot occupancy rates and improves the overall parking experience on campus. It demonstrates a cost-effective and efficient solution to parking management.

Conclusion

SlugSpot is a testament to the effective use of technology in solving practical problems. It showcases the potential of sensor-based systems in urban planning and management.
