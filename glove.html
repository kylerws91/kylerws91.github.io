ECE167 Coil Launcher

Coil Launcher: Precision Through Sensors

Integrating mechanical design, motor control, and glove interface

Project Overview

This project explores the development of a sensor-based coil launcher controlled via a glove with an IMU module and a capacitive touch sensor, focusing on accurate pitch and yaw control.

Design and Control

Details the integration of mechanical, electrical, and software components, including a two-degree-of-freedom turret mechanism and DC motors with quadrature encoders for precise positioning.

Challenges and Solutions

Highlights the challenges faced during development and the iterative approach adopted to refine the system, demonstrating practical application of sensor technologies.

Conclusion

Summarizes the project's success in creating a functional, sensor-based coil launcher, emphasizing the importance of iterative testing and problem-solving.
